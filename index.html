The Four NVC Components

1. Observation

Describe what happened without judgment or interpretation.

2. Feeling

Share how you feel in response to the observation.

3. Need

Connect your feeling to an unmet need or value.

4. Request

Ask for a clear, positive action to meet your need.

Example

Observation: When I saw the report arrive late...

Feeling: I felt stressed and frustrated.

Need: I need reliability and timely communication.

Request: Would you be willing to send me an update sooner next time?

Tips for compassionate communication

  • Focus on observations, not evaluations.
  • Express feelings honestly, without blaming.
  • Connect to needs rather than demands.
  • Phrase requests positively and clearly.
